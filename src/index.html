Welcome to this glimpse into my life. To better understand me, I consider myself the king of the misfits. Since my youth, I've never conformed to things as they are; I've always been reinventing and creating new games or 'fixing' existing ones. This has shaped my personality into a curious one, always questioning why things are the way they are and imagining how I could do them differently. I tend to overthink, but when others see a disadvantage, I see opportunities to think of more possibilities and build better solutions or prepare for potential challenges. My educational journey has revolved around creating things, with a focus on business and technology companies. The other side of my education is deeply rooted in technology, which I love as a tool to materialize my thoughts into the real world. I'm always developing a thesis around the question: 'Why is this useful for humanity?' and experiencing the satisfaction of being right about my hypotheses, knowing that people genuinely desire what I create. It's a thrilling feeling—akin to a drug. There's nothing quite like being correct in a complex thesis from my mind and leading a group of people to victory. Some may see it as mere work, but to me, building a solution feels like being an artist, combining design efficiency and emotions to create a masterpiece.
